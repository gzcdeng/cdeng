Hello I'm

Chengbin Deng

I am an Assistant Professor in Department of Geography at the State University of New York at Binghamton. My research strives to Analyze emerging geospatial data and generate new data by developing cutting-edge remote sensing image processing algorithms, Discover new phenomena and to develop new theories built on the derived new data and Improve and advance the understanding of human-environment interactions.
